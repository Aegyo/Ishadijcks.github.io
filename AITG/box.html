Reward Disabling

Should there be a reward you no longer wish to receive, you may disable it by simply clicking on it. Should that reward be rolled by the game, you will receive a 1% refund for the box.
Restrictions:

  • Only revealed prizes can be disabled.
  • Attendance Box rewards cannot be disabled.
  • Supply Crate rewards cannot be disabled.
  • Gambler’s Box “Nothing” reward cannot be disabled.
