Objective

The purpose of the Fishing feature is to… fish.

Basically, you just wait until you get a catch (the stickman animation changes and a triangle appears at the bottom right bar). Wait until the triangle reaches the red line then press any key on your keyboard. If you are accurate, you will get perfect catches for bonus EXP (20% ~ 120%, based on streak) and extra Fishing Mastery (1 ~ 20, based on streak). Perfect catches also give you 50% less Fatigue. When you have a perfect streak, fish also appear faster.

When you catch items, you gain EXP (affected by your current level, EXP Boost and other EXP multipliers), and the items go to the bucket.

You can click the bucket to open your “inventory” and use the items you got in Fishing. You can hold the SHIFT key to use things faster. You can keep a maximum of 1,337 items in your bucket, if your bucket is full, you will be unable to fish. You can increase the capacity of your bucket to carry more items.

Items

Junk

  • Chance of appearing: 60% ~ 65%
  • Purpose:
    • Recycle: You can recycle junk items for Crafting Material (1k ~ 50k)
    • Sell: You can sell junk items for Coins (Lv. 8 or higher junk only)
  • Movement speed: 3
  • EXP rate: 1,000 ~ 3,900
  • Fatigue: 10 ~ 20
  • Mastery: 11 ~ 50

Fish

  • Chance of appearing: 35%
  • Purpose:
    • Eat: You can eat fish to recover Fatigue (1 ~ 9) - limit 100 per day. Only usable when Fatigue is 150 or above.
    • Sell: You can sell fish for Coins
    • Feed: You can feed fish to your pet for Pet Fullness (Lv. 8 or higher fish only) - limit 50 per day. Lv. 30 fish gives 2 Fullness, all other ones give 1. Only usable when Pet Fullness is 80 or below.
  • Movement speed: 4
  • EXP rate: 2,000 ~ 7,800
  • Fatigue: 20 ~ 40
  • Mastery: 22 ~ 100

Fatigue

Some of the stuff in this section is outdated.

Fatigue affects how frequent things appear in Fishing. For every 50 Fatigue you get, things appear 1% less frequently. When your Fatigue is 5,000, you cannot fish.
Your Fatigue is decreased by 5 every 5 minutes you play (or 60 every 60 minutes you don’t play), and is reset when the day ends.
You can also eat fish (up to 100 per day) and drink Energy Drinks to reduce your Fatigue.

Treasure Box Prizes

If you have a Treasure Box and a Key of the same type, you can open the Treasure Box to get a random prize. All prizes have equal chance (exception: Golden Fishing Rod, which is a one-time reward and has a 5% chance). When you open a box, 1 Treasure Box and 1 Key is consumed.

  • Bronze Treasure Box
    • 1 Silver Treasure Box
    • 1 Silver Key
    • 2 Regular Energy Drink
    • 2 Regular Pet Food
    • 200 Fishing Mastery
    • 500 Blue Coins
  • Silver Treasure Box
    • 1 Golden Treasure Box
    • 1 Golden Key
    • 5 Regular Energy Drink
    • 5 Regular Pet Food
    • 1,000 Fishing Mastery
    • 2,500 Blue Coins
  • Golden Treasure Box
    • 5 Elite Energy Drink
    • 5 Elite Pet Food
    • 4,000 Fishing Mastery
    • 10,000 Blue Coins

Fishing Rods

Fishing Rods are a new addition in v1456. You can unlock Fishing Rods by completing various tasks. Each Fishing Rod as a unique effect.

  • #1 - Basic Fishing Rod
    • Unlock: Unlocked for free when you get the feature.
    • Effect: +5 Fishing Mastery per successful catch.
  • #2 - Improved Fishing Rod
    • Unlock: Raise your Fishing Skill to 15.
    • Effect: Higher chance to catch good fish / junk items.
  • #3 - Golden Fishing Rod
    • Unlock: 5% chance from Golden Treasure Box.
    • Effect: When you catch a Treasure Box or a Key, it is guaranteed to be Golden.
  • #4 - Cursed Fishing Rod
    • Unlock: Reach a Perfect Streak of 25x or higher.
    • Effect: Activates a harder mode, but if you get a perfect catch, you gain additional EXP, score and have a higher chance to catch better fish / junk items.
  • #5 - Anti-Fish Fishing Rod
    • Unlock: Purchase in the Mystery Shop for 250,000 Blue Coins.
    • Effect: Allows you to catch only junk and special items.
  • #6 - Anti-Junk Fishing Rod
    • Unlock: Purchase in the Mystery Shop for 350,000 Blue Coins.
    • Effect: Allows you to catch only fish and special items.
  • #7 - Idler’s Fishing Rod
    • Unlock: Raise your Fishing Skill to 30.
    • Effect: Increases your chance to catch items when idling by 20%.
  • #8 - Easy-Mode Fishing Rod
    • Unlock: Achieve 50,000,000 score in a day.
    • Effect: Slows the triangle down until your perfect streak reaches 15x.
  • #9 - Rainbow Fishing Rod
    • Unlock: Achieve a total score of 1,000,000,000.
    • Effect: Greatly increases the spawn rate of all items.
  • #10 - Mysterious Fishing Rod
    • Unlock: 20,000 perfect catches or 7 other rods unlocked.
    • Effect: Increases chance to catch Treasure Boxes / Keys. Chance to stop you from catching Treasure Boxes if you have more Treasure Boxes than Keys, and vice-versa.

Score / Milestones

When you fish, you will gain Score based on how close you are to the red line. Perfect catches give more Score. Score is also affected by Mastery gain, so the Career also affects your score.
When you reach milestone scores (daily / total), you will gain extra Fishing Mastery and some bonuses.

Skill / Mastery

When you fish, you will gain Fishing Mastery. When you get enough Fishing Mastery, you can pay Coins to raise your Fishing Skill. Note that if you don’t improve your Fishing Skill, you will stop gaining Fishing Mastery.

When you skill up, things will appear more frequently and you have a chance to catch higher level fish / junk items.

Also, as of v1456, every 3 times you raise your Fishing Skill, the red perfect line will stay for 1 streak longer.

LEGEND System

As of a recent update, you can now increase your Fishing Skill past 30. Each Fishing Skill past 30 still increases fish frequency and chance to catch better items as usual, but also gives you a lot of additional EXP! You will receive 25% bonus EXP at Skill 31, +5% more bonus EXP for each Skill, for a maximum of 120% bonus EXP at Skill 50!

But, be sure to fish frequently to keep your LEGEND status, as your Fishing Skill will drop by 1 every day if it is higher than 30.

Idling

You can catch fish while idling by doing nothing. If you touch the keyboard and the triangle is too far away from the red line, it will result in failure. You have a base chance of 30% to catch fish when idling, and this chance is modified by the difference between the fish / junk item’s level and your Fishing Skill (1 level = 3%). Additionally, if you have the Fisher Career maxed out, this chance is increased by 10%.

Career

This feature has a Career. You can activate it in the Career screen, of course.

  • Activation Bonus (per level):
    • EXP gain in Fishing: +0.5%
    • Fishing Mastery: +1%
    • 0.4% chance to gain no Fatigue when fishing
  • Level 100 Bonus:
    • Chance to catch items when idling is increased by 10%.
  • Level 200 Bonus:
    • Chance to catch special items is increased.

Elite Fisher Card

You can obtain Elite Fisher Cards by opening Legendary Boxes! Use an Elite Fisher Card in the Cards screen to gain 1.5x EXP from Fishing and have a better chance to catch high level fish/junk items, as well as an increased chance to catch Mystery Boxes!

FAQ

Q. How do I fish?
A. You clearly didn’t read the guide if you have to ask this. But anyway, press any key or simply idle.


Q. My perfect streak breaks when I catch “nothing”. HELP!!!
A. No. Your perfect streak broke because your timing was off. Sometimes, that can result in a “nothing” outcome. Even if you got something, your perfect streak would still break anyway.


Q. Why is it that the game only recognizes it 0.5 seconds after I press the key? It makes fishing impossible.
A. Try to reduce quality and use anti-lag options.


Q. How do I get higher streaks?
A. Practice. Practice makes perfect. You can also just stop caring about the achievements for a while, you’ll perform better when you are relaxed.
