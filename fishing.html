Objective
The purpose of the Fishing feature is to... fish.
Basically, you just wait until you get a catch (the stickman animation changes and a triangle appears at the bottom right bar). Wait until the triangle reaches the red line then press any key on your keyboard. If you are accurate, you will get perfect catches for bonus EXP (20% ~ 120%, based on streak) and extra Fishing Mastery (1 ~ 20, based on streak). Perfect catches also give you 50% less Fatigue. When you have a perfect streak, fish also appear faster.
When you catch items, you gain EXP (affected by your current level, EXP Boost and other EXP multipliers), and the items go to the bucket.
You can click the bucket to open your “inventory” and use the items you got in Fishing. You can hold the SHIFT key to use things faster. You can keep a maximum of 1,337 items in your bucket – if your bucket is full, you will be unable to fish. You can increase the capacity of your bucket to carry more items.
